Green Chilaquiles

Description

Chilaquiles are a traditional Mexican dish of fried tortillas bathed in green or red salsa (depending on the region) until tender. Chilaquiles are most commonly eaten at breakfast time.

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 18 5" corn tortillas
  • 2 pounds green tomatillos husked and rinsed
  • 1/2 of a large white onion
  • 1 or 2 serrano or jalapeño chiles
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 or 3 cilantro sprigs
  • 2 cups of vegetable or chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil plus more for brushing tortillas

To Garnish:

  • 1/2 cup onion thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup of cilantro chopped
  • 1/2 cup queso fresco or cotija or substitute with Farmer's cheese or mild feta, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup mexican cream

Steps

To prepare the tortillas:

  1. Heat the oven to 300 degrees
  2. Cut the tortillas into 2-inch, bite sized pieces
  3. Lightly brush with oil, sprinkle salt
  4. Set them on a baking tray and bake in the oven until crispy, about 15 to 20 minutes
  5. Let the pieces cool

Alternately, you can fry the tortilla pieces

To prepare the tomatillo sauce:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Place the tomatillos, onions, garlic and serrano chiles in a bowl
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil and rub all the ingredients.
  4. Lay the ingredients in a baking tray, sprinkle with salt
  5. Bake until the tomatillos are soft and plump and all the ingredients look charred
  6. Let the ingredients cool
  7. Add the charred vegetables, the cilantro and the broth to a blender and mix well
  8. Heat a pan over medium heat, adding one additional tablespoon of oil
  9. When the oil is hot, add the sauce from the blender and finish cooking over medium heat for about 10 minutes
  10. Season to taste

To serve:

  1. When the sauce is hot, quickly but carefully add the tortillas
  2. Stir the tortillas into the mixture so that they are fully coated with the sauce
  3. Serve the tortillas and salsa in a large platter
  4. Garnish with the sliced onions, crumbled mexican cheese, drizzle with the cream
